Hall Of Achievements
Within my 2 years of being in an arts club at school, I joined a lot of activities that they offered.
There were several art competitions, and I was really active in the community!
Other than the competitions, I also got art-related awards outside of the club such as my 1st place award for best book cover art, and 1st place for best radio drama poster.
Back to the arts club, we also occasionally sold stickers, keychains, and other trinkets at our booth at club fairs! I'm proud to say I have made art for those booths and even managed it when the club officers weren't around (shoutout my senior ates and kuyas ily guys thank you for everything)
Recently, I also got the chance to have my art sold as part of my school's 60th anniversary :DD
