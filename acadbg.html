Academic Background
From Casa (Kinder) to Gr.6, I studied in O.B Montessori Center Sta. Ana. From there, even at an early age, I had an interest in art. I drew my classmates, teachers, and even took up my first commission in Gr.6.
That early interest led to my love of art today, and my drive to study it and use it as my personal outlet to voice my thoughts to the world.
From Gr. 7-8, I studied in Manila Science Highschool. It was a change of pace, surely, but at the same time, I joined the MSHS Arts Club, there. I met a community worth of people who were interested in art in the way I was, and that drove me to compete in the competitions they offered.
From there, I learned many things about art, making merchaindise, and making art for advertisements. I learned how to capture the eye of both artists, who have a deep appreciation for technique, and non-artists, who need something more eye catching to draw them in.
Now, currently, in Gr.9, i'm back in O.B.M.C, because aside from being an artist and student, I am also an athelte. Swimming conflicted with the brutal schedule of MaSci, so I came back to O.B.M.C to balance both my studies, sport, and myself, in my art.
Even if i'm still young compared to some of the artists around me, I strive to improve and learn as much as I can.
